Website About My Favorite Albums

  • Blonde by Frank Ocean
  • Ctrl by SZA
  • Freudian by Daniel Caesar
  • The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill by Lauryn Hill

Why I favor each of these albums?

  • Blonde - I love the nuances each of the songs have. Each song is captivating and a joy to listen to. None of the songs are bad, I could listen to this album for hours and hours.
  • Ctrl - This album has been my go to album since it's came out. I love that each song brings a new layer of curiousity and attentiveness. SZA never fails to make music that is so realtable and beautiful.
  • Freudian - Every song brings out such a strong, yet precise, emotion. I could cry to a lot of the songs just because of how alluring they are. This is another album that has no skips, I love everything about this album.
  • The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill - This album was so beautifully curated. Every song and every lyric was perfectly deliberated. The soul and effort that was put into making this mastry is truely appreciated!
